This Letter of Intent ("LOI") outlines the preliminary interest of **[Corporate Buyer Company Name]** ("Buyer") in exploring a strategic partnership and utilizing the B2B logistics infrastructure developed by **Agrocart Technologies Ltd.** ("Agrocart").

### 1. Context and Mutual Interest
We recognize the significant challenges within the current agricultural supply chain, particularly regarding post-harvest loss, lack of supply provenance, and opaque logistics data. Having reviewed the capabilities of the Agrocart V1.0 Platform—specifically the **Quality Vault Auditing**, **Public Traceability Ledgers**, and **Digital Escrow mechanisms**—we believe your solution aligns with our internal goals of securing a transparent, reliable, and ESG-compliant supply chain.

### 2. Proposed Scope of Collaboration
Provided that Agrocart successfully launches its physical hub operations and meets our standard vendor compliance and operational requirements, **[Corporate Buyer Company Name]** expresses the intent to:

*   **Pilot Phase:** Route an initial volume of approximately **[X,000] Metric Tons** of **[Crop Type, e.g., Maize/Cassava]** through the Agrocart logistics network during the upcoming harvest season.
*   **System Integration:** Evaluate the integration of Agrocart’s Corporate Procurement Dashboard and API to streamline our internal inventory tracking and sustainability reporting.
*   **Network Mandate:** Encourage our existing network of aggregators and suppliers to adopt the Agrocart platform to ensure standardized quality grading and delivery verification.

### 3. Non-Binding Nature
This LOI is intended solely to express the current intentions of the parties and to facilitate further discussions. With the exception of any confidentiality obligations previously agreed upon, **this document does not constitute a legally binding contract, obligation, or commitment** by either party to enter into a definitive agreement or to execute any transactions. Any formal partnership will be subject to successful pilot testing, legal review, and the execution of a definitive Commercial Service Agreement.

We look forward to monitoring your progress as you deploy physical pilot hubs and to advancing our discussions in the near future.
