# Agrocart: Data Room & Fundraising Checklist

When an investor decides they like your Pitch Deck, they will say: *"Send me access to your Data Room."* 

A Data Room is simply a secure Google Drive or Dropbox folder containing all the legal, financial, and operational documents that prove your business is real, compliant, and ready to scale. 

Here is the master checklist of documents you need to prepare:

## 1. Company & Legal Documents
These prove that Agrocart legally exists and the founders actually own the IP.
- [ ] **Certificate of Incorporation** (e.g., CAC documents in Nigeria or Delaware C-Corp if registered in the US).
- [ ] **Memorandum and Articles of Association (MEMART).**
- [ ] **Founder's Agreement** (Outlines equity splits and vesting schedules between co-founders).
- [ ] **IP Assignment Agreements** (A legal document stating that the code you wrote belongs to "Agrocart Inc." and not you personally).

## 2. Financial Documents
These prove your math and your current cash position.
- [ ] **The Financial Model** (An Excel version of the `AGROCART_FINANCIAL_MODEL.md` showing 3-year projections).
- [ ] **The Cap Table** (Capitalization Table - an Excel sheet showing exactly who owns what percentage of the company, including any early angels and an option pool for future employees).
- [ ] **Current Bank Statements** (If you have already started spending money or generating early revenue).

## 3. Product & Technology
These prove that your software is real, secure, and scalable.
- [ ] **Technical Architecture Diagram** (A visual map showing Next.js, Firebase, Africa's Talking API, etc. We have this documented in your `PROJECT_CHRONICLE.md`).
- [ ] **Product Roadmap** (What you plan to build in the next 12-18 months. E.g., The Fintech integration, Bluetooth scales).
- [ ] **Demo Video** (A 2-minute screen recording of a Hub Manager taking in a crop and dispatching it to a Transporter).

## 4. Market & Strategy
These prove you know how to win the market.
- [ ] **The Pitch Deck** (PDF format).
- [ ] **Executive Summary / One-Pager** (PDF format).
- [ ] **Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy Document** (A detailed tactical plan on *exactly* which city you are launching in first, how many agents you are hiring, and how you will convince the first flour mill to use your platform).
- [ ] **Competitor Matrix** (A deeper version of the `AGROCART_MARKET_RESEARCH.md`).

## 5. Traction & Validation (Crucial for Pre-Seed)
Since you might not have massive revenue yet, you need "Validation" documents.
- [ ] **Letters of Intent (LOIs)** (A non-binding letter from a corporate buyer saying: *"If Agrocart can guarantee traceability and quality, we intend to route $X amount of our logistics through them."* - This is the single most powerful document you can have at this stage).
- [ ] **Pilot Results** (If you have run a small test with 5 trucks, a 1-page case study on how it went).

---
**Advice:** Create a Google Drive folder named `Agrocart_Data_Room_2026`. Create 5 sub-folders matching the categories above. Put the documents in there and set it to "View Only." When an investor asks, you just send them one link.
